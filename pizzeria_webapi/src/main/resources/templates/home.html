Welcome to the Admin Panel

This is your central hub for managing everything related to your pizzeria’s menu. Here, you can:

🍕 View All Pizzas – See a complete list of pizzas currently on the menu.
➕ Add New Pizzas – Create delicious new pizza options with custom ingredients and prices.
✏️ Edit Pizzas – Update descriptions, adjust prices, or tweak ingredients anytime.
🗑️ Delete Pizzas – Remove outdated or discontinued pizzas from the menu.
🆕 New Feature – You can now add special offers.

Easily keep your menu fresh and exciting with just a few clicks.
